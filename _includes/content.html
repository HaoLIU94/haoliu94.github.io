Welcome

I am Hao Liu, a Machine Learning Engineer specialized in Computer Vision at Navya since March 2019. After graduated with a master degree in Machine Learning from Université Paris-Saclay, I have spent four years in the industry to work on computer vision problems applied in autonomous driving. My interests are in Object detection, Semantic segmentation, Multi-object tracking, State estimation, Sensor fusion, Simultaneous localization and mapping.
